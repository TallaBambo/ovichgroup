Vision

To become the world's most trusted and well-known media company by 2025.
Our vision form the basis of every action we take.

In a complex society where information is an essential tool in making people participate in the life of the community, we bridge the gap between the information-rich and the information-poor by giving a voice to the voiceless and granting opportunities for those facing injustice to share their stories. We bear witness to happenings putting the people’s interest at the heart of every story we share.
